This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you:

* Use our website at **jolix.io** and any subdomains (the "**Site**")

## 2. Information we collect

We collect information in three main ways:

* **Information you provide to us directly**
* **Information collected automatically**
* **Information from third-party services**

### 2.1. Information you provide to us

When you create or use a Jolix account, you may provide:

* **Account information**
  Name, email address, password or login method, profile photo or avatar (optional), time zone, country, language settings.

* **Freelancer / business profile**
  Business name, logo, brand colors, services offered, rates, niche, bio, portfolio links, social handles (e.g. X, LinkedIn, website).

* **Client & project data**
  Client names, emails, business details, project titles, timelines, tasks, files, messages, notes, and internal comments.

* **Financial & billing data**
  Billing name, address, country, payment-related info handled by third-party processors (e.g. Stripe, PayPal), invoices, transaction history, subscription plan.

> We do **not** store your full credit card number. Payment details are processed and stored by our payment processors.

* **Content you generate**
  Proposals, contracts, invoices, forms, portfolio content, case studies, social posts you draft inside Jolix, and AI-assisted content (prompts and outputs).

* **Support communications**
  Messages you send to our support team, feedback, bug reports, and feature requests.

### 2.2. Information we collect automatically

When you use Jolix, we may automatically collect:

* **Usage data**
  Pages and screens you visit; features you use (e.g. leads, proposals, scheduling); buttons you click; session duration and timestamps.

* **Device and technical data**
  IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referral URLs, basic geographic region (city/country level), log files, and error reports.

We may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to remember your session, keep you logged in, and understand how Jolix is used.

You can manage cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if you disable them.

### 2.3. Information from third-party services

If you connect Jolix to third-party accounts, we may receive limited information from them, depending on what you authorize. This can include:

* **Payment providers** (e.g. Stripe, PayPal)
  Payment confirmations, subscription status, limited card info, country.

* **Calendar & email providers**
  Basic calendar events and metadata needed for scheduling and reminders.

* **Social platforms** (e.g. LinkedIn; for X we may only offer manual posting via intent links)
  Authorization tokens to post content on your behalf and basic profile info (name, profile URL).

* **Cloud storage / integrations** (future)
  Access tokens and metadata required to connect to your files or notes.

We request the minimum permissions needed to provide the relevant feature and only use that data for the purposes described in this policy.

## 4. AI features and your data

Jolix uses AI to help you generate social media posts, draft proposals and emails, and provide suggestions and templates.

When you use AI features:

* Some of your input (prompts, context) and outputs may be sent to our AI providers to generate responses.
* We limit what is sent to what's needed for the feature to work.
* We do **not** sell your data to third-party AI companies.

Where possible, we use providers and configurations that do **not** train their models on your identifiable personal data. See our AI documentation for more details as these features evolve.

You can choose which content you send through AI features. You are responsible for ensuring you have the right to process your clients' data in this way.
